COVID-19

The year 2020 has taken a turn that no one could have expected. The world is slowing down and joining forces to fight a virus that we cannot see. Schools are shut down, public places banned, and the global death count is reaching 100,000. If you are reading this (even after this is all over), I encourage you to help others in every way you can. Now is not the time to worry about the "I"; it's time to worry about the "we".

You can monitor the virus with the COVID-19 tracking website that Johns Hopkins is running. At the time of this writing, there are 1,413,415 confirmed cases with 81,200 deaths around the world.

If you would like to help in this fight, there are many nonprofits you can donate to, like Hope Worldwide.

Please stay safe.

Last updated April 7, 2020, from the United States.
